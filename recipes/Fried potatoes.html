Fried potatoes

A fast and tasty food

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Vegetal oil
  • 2 potatoes
  • Napkins

Steps

  1. Serve 1 cup of oil in a boiling oil and turn on the stove
  2. cut the potatoes into strips and put them on the oil, wait 2 minutes untill the are already fried and get them off from the hot oil
  3. then serve them on a napkin to remove the oil excess
