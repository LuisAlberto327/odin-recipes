Butter steal

A fast and healthy dish

Ingredients

  • 1 fillet
  • 100 butter grams
  • aluminium paper
  • Pepper
  • English sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic

Steps

  1. Wrap the fillet with the aluminium paper but don't close it yet
  2. Cut the clove of garlic into a little pieces
  3. Add some drops of english sauce and pepper
  4. Add the garlic pieces and spread the butter on the fillet
  5. Close the fillet with the paper
  6. Heat a griddle with low fire and put the fillet
  7. Wait 15 minutes, put it upside down and wait another 15 minutes
