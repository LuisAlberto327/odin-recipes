Hot cakes

A tasty start of a good day

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fluor for hot cakes
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 400 butter grams
  • 1 glass bowl

Steps

  1. Serve the three ingredientes in the glass bowl untill get an even dough
  2. Pick a hand blender and mix them all
  3. Heat a frying pan with low fire and wet it with the butter
  4. Serve the dough, wait 40 seconds and put it upside down for another 40 seconds
  5. Serve it on a dish and add butter and honey to taste
