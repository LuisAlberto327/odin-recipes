Cheese Cake

The perfect dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 aluminium bowl
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 500 curd grams
  • Vanilla cookies
  • 90 butter grams

Steps

  1. Triturate the cokies untill get powdered
  2. Melt the butter and mix it with the powdered cookies until you get an even dough
  3. Serve them on a napkin to remove the oil excess
  4. Cover the bowl with the dough and press to flatten the dough against the bowl
  5. Pour the two milks, the eggs, the curd in a blender and mix
  6. Pour the blend inside the bowl
  7. Get the bowl inside the oven for 45 minutes and set a temperature of 180 C°/356 F°
  8. Take out the bowl and wait 30 minutes untill cold down
